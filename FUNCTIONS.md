pgopenssltypes functions
========================

Digest
------

The digest functions take text and return a hexadecimal encoding of
the message digest value. Some functions may not be supported by the
installed OpenSSL library.

 * dgst_md4(text)
 * dgst_md5(text)
 * dgst_sha(text)
 * dgst_sha1(text)
 * dgst_sha224(text)
 * dgst_sha256(text)
 * dgst_sha384(text)
 * dgst_sha512(text)
 * dgst_ripedm160(text)
 * dgst_whirlpool(text)
